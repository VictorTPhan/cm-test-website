In order to ensure interaction and classroom quality, all courses are conducted in small classes (2-3 people) in the form of live broadcast, or 1-on-1 according to needs.

At the present, most of the programming courses in the advanced section are 16 class hours each, and each class hour is 1.5 hours. If scheduled once a week, it takes 16 weeks to complete a course. Our general arrangement for each course is to learn basic knowledge points in the first 10 weeks, focus on completing an independent project in the last 6 weeks, and then use the completed project to participate in the competition at the end of each semester.

The purpose of learning programming at this stage is not only to learn programming itself, but also to help students achieve some results and prepare for future applications. Therefore, for each course, we will lead students to complete their own projects and then participate in competitions. Now we push each student to participate in the competition at least twice a year.

Small classes or 1-to-1 courses will open new classes every week. After the trial class is completed, we will arrange a suitable new class for the students. We will communicate with parents during the class time. Once a week or multiple times is fine, because it is a small class. Therefore, it can be coordinated according to the specific time situation of each student.
